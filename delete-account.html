Data Safety

How to delete my account

What are the data we are capturing with your Gavesha account.
  • Email address
  • First name, last name
  • Mobile number [if provided]
  • Location data [only for weather widget users]
  • Language preference
  • Android os version

Location data is only captured in the weather widget. We are only using location data with weather related data and not using for any other purposes.

Deleting your account will remove all the above captured data except below details

  • Email address: We need to keep your email address for response your inquiries regarding account deletion. We will not use this information for any other purposes.
  • Location data captured with weather widget. We will remove your email address reference and any other personal identification details from the captured weather data.
Please follow the below steps to delete your account and related data.

1. Open profile page
 
 

2. Go to bottom of the profile view and click on delete button.

3. Type DELETE in the text box and click on DELETE ACCOUNT button.

4. Check the message to confirm.
